Company Leave Policy

📌 Types of Leave

  • Sick Leave: For health-related issues.
  • Casual Leave: For personal work or short breaks.
  • Earned Leave: Accumulated leave for long vacations.

📅 Leave Entitlement

  • 12 Casual Leaves per year
  • 10 Sick Leaves per year
  • 12 Earned leaves per year, unused leaves can be carried forward

📝 Leave Application Rules

  • Apply at least 2 days in advance (except emergencies)
  • Provide valid reason for leave
  • Manager approval is mandatory

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Unapproved leaves may lead to salary deduction
  • Fake reasons can lead to disciplinary action
